# Content Categories

Everything in the hub falls into one of 8 categories.

## Content Categories

These categories contain the actual source material — episodes, articles, transcripts.

### Podcast
Latent Space podcast episodes. Deep dives with industry leaders on AI engineering topics. Each node has the full transcript, guest connections, and topic edges.

### Article
Blog posts from the latent.space Substack. Written analysis, tutorials, and essays by Swyx and guest authors.

### AI News
Daily AINews digests from smol.ai. Each digest covers the most important AI developments of the day.

### Builders Club
Meetup recordings from the Latent Space Builders Club. Community members presenting their projects and builds.

### Paper Club
Deep-dive paper discussions. The community reads and discusses important AI research papers together.

### Workshop
Conference talks, tutorials, and presentations from AI Engineer events (World's Fair, Code Summit, etc.).

## People & Things

### Guest
People who appear on or create Latent Space content — podcast guests, conference speakers, article authors. Connected to the content they appear in via edges.

### Entity
Organizations (OpenAI, Anthropic, LangChain) and technical topics (RAG, agents, fine-tuning). These nodes serve as connection hubs — many content nodes link to them.

## How to Use Categories

- **Dashboard** shows all 8 categories with preview items
- **Sidebar** lets you filter by category
- **Search** can be scoped to a specific category
- **MCP tools** support `node_type` filtering

## Sorting

Content categories (podcast, article, ainews, etc.) sort by **most recent** — newest content first.

People/entity categories (guest, entity) sort by **most connected** — nodes with the most edges first. This surfaces the most central figures and topics.
